FAQ

Q: How do I add a widget?

Long-press your Home Screen, tap "+" in the top left, then search "OshiTimer" and add it.

Q: Live Activity is not showing

Go to Settings, then OshiTimer, and make sure Live Activities is enabled.

Q: How do I remove ads?

Tap the "x" button next to the ad banner to purchase ad removal.
